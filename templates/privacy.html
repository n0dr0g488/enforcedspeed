Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 11, 2026
Last Updated: February 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Mindra LLC, a Wyoming limited liability company (“Mindra,” “Company,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, and discloses information in connection with the EnforcedSpeed platform available at www.enforcedspeed.com (the “Platform”).

This Privacy Policy is incorporated by reference into the Terms of Service.

1. SCOPE

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through the Platform in connection with the Services. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that may be linked from the Platform.

2. DEFINITIONS

For purposes of this Policy:

“Account” means your registered user account (email + password login).

“Ticket Data” means the structured fields submitted in the “Submit a Ticket” flow (e.g., state, road, posted speed, ticketed speed, overage, date, notes, and any derived fields such as verification flags and confidence indicators).

“Ticket Image” means an uploaded photo or scan associated with a ticket submission.

“Service Providers” means third-party companies that process information on our behalf (examples listed below).

“Personal Information” means information that identifies, relates to, describes, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked (directly or indirectly) with an individual or household.

3. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

3.1 Information You Provide

You may provide information when you:

Create an account

Submit ticket information

Communicate with us

This may include:

Email address or account identifier,

Ticket-related details (jurisdiction, speeds, dates),

Optionally uploaded Ticket Images.

3.2 Automated Collection

When you access the Services, we (and our infrastructure providers) may automatically collect:

Log and device data such as IP address, user-agent, browser type, operating system, timestamps, pages requested, and referring/exit pages.

Usage data such as interactions with pages/features, error reports, and performance diagnostics, etc.

Approximate location that is derived from your IP address for security and basic service functionality.

Where information is collected from third parties that collection is subject to their terms.

4. UPLOAD AND AUTOMATED PROCESSING OF TICKET IMAGES

The process of uploading Ticket Images is detailed in our Terms of Service, that you accept prior to uploading a Ticket Image. The Terms of Service further describe the steps Company takes when conducting Automated Processing.

5. INFORMATION WE RETAIN

After image deletion, we may retain:

Structured ticket data (state, road, speeds, dates),

Verification status indicators,

Internal processing markers.

We do not retain:

Ticket Images,

Names, dates of birth, driver’s license numbers, other personal information

Biometric identifiers.

6. HOW WE USE INFORMATION

We use information to:

Provide and operate the Services (account login, ticket submission, feeds, maps, and analytics).

Conduct Automated Processing of Ticket Images

Maintain security, prevent abuse, troubleshoot, and monitor performance.

Improve and develop features, including generating aggregated and de‑identified insights (e.g., enforcement “strictness” rankings, road-level trends, confidence/density signals).

Communicate with you (service announcements, account notices, password resets, support).

7. SHARING OF INFORMATION

7.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We use third parties to host and operate the Services. These currently include:

Render

Redis

Cloudflare

Google Cloud Vision® and Google Maps

SendGrid

7.2 Aggregated / De-identified Information

We may share aggregated or de-identified Ticket Data (e.g., statistics, rankings, trends) that cannot reasonably be used to identify you.

We do not sell personal information.

7.3 Legal, Safety, and Business Transfers

We may share information to comply with the law, enforce our terms, protect rights/safety, respond to lawful requests, or as part of a merger, acquisition, financing, reorganization, or sale of assets.

8. AUTOMATED DECISION-MAKING

The Services use Automated Processing to assist with data verification. They do not make legal determinations or decisions with legal or similarly significant effects.

9. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Cookies are governed by the Cookies Policy which is incorporated herein.

10. DATA SECURITY

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no system is completely secure.

11. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:

Request access to personal information,

Request deletion of personal information,

Correct inaccurate information.

Requests may be subject to technical and legal limitations.

12. CHILDREN’S PRIVACY

The Services are intended for users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are under 18, do not use the Services.

13. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last Updated” date will reflect changes. Your continued use of the Platform and/or Services after the effective date of revised terms constitutes acceptance of the changes.
